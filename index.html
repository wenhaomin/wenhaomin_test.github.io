About me

I received the B.S. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University, in 2019. I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate in School of Computer and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University. And I am in institute of network science and intelligent systems (INSIS), supervised by Youfang Lin and Huaiyu Wan. Currently, I'm at the School of Computing of NUS as a joint Ph.D. student, supervised by Roger Zimmermann and Yuxuan Liang. My main research interests include data mining, spatial-temporal data forecasting, applied machine learning. I focus on using AI to solve practical problems.
